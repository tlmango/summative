How does computer use harm the environment?

Using a screen for 8 years results in greenhouse gas emissions equivalent to 70g CO2e from energy consumption.

Common setups and their carbon footprints include:

  • Desktop + screen: 621kg CO2e
  • Laptop + screen: 691kg CO2e (+11%)
  • Desktop + 2 screens: 903kg CO2e (+45%)
  • Laptop + screen at office + screen at home: 928kg CO2e (+49%)
  • Desktop + screen + laptop: 1,030kg CO2e (+66%)

The average desktop consumes 40W of electricity, which contributes to CO2 emissions.
